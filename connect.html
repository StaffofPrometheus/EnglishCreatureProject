  • Connection to Frankenstein (current)

This game connects to Frankenstein in multiple ways. First, the trial is about whether the father is guilty of neglect of a child that committed murder. This relates to how Frankenstein may be guilty of neglect of the Creature; the Creature killed multiple people. Another way this game relates to Frankenstein is that Justine Moritz was put on trial (and found guilty) for the murder of WIlliam.
